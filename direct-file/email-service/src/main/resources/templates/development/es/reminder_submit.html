Direct File

Submit your 2024 federal tax return by April 15

You started a federal tax return using Direct File but didn't finish it.

If you already resubmitted or filed another way and the IRS accepted your federal tax return, you don't need to do anything.

April 15 at 11:59 PM in your time zone (or April 17 at 11:59 PM for Massachusetts residents) is the federal tax filing deadline. This is also the last day to use Direct File for this filing season. Sign in to Direct File to continue your federal tax return and submit before the deadline.

What if I need more time?

You can apply for an extension by April 15 (or April 17 for Massachusetts residents). You won't be able to use Direct File to file an extension. 

If you have an extension or plan to file after the federal tax filing deadline, you can't use Direct File. You'll need to find another way to file your federal taxes. Remember, even if you have an extension to file your taxes, you still need to pay your taxes by the due date to avoid penalties. 

Don't forget about state taxes and credits

Depending on your situation, you may have to file a state tax return in addition to a federal tax return. You may also be eligible for state tax credits.

Keep due dates for state tax returns in mind. Some state tax filing tools may not allow you to electronically file your state tax return until your federal tax return has been accepted.
