Your 2024 federal tax return is submitted

What's next?

You'll get an email from Direct File once your federal tax return has been accepted or rejected—which may take up to an hour.

  • If your federal tax return is accepted, you'll receive instructions to pay your tax balance or check the status of your tax refund
  • If your federal tax return is rejected, you'll receive instructions to fix your tax return and resubmit or find other ways to file your taxes

For help, sign in to Direct File and chat online with customer support (available through April 20).

Don't forget about state taxes and credits

Depending on your situation, you may have to file a state tax return in addition to a federal tax return. You may also be eligible for state tax credits. Sign in to Direct File to learn more.

The state tax filing deadline is April 15 at 11:59 PM in your timezone (or April 17 at 11:59 PM for Massachusetts residents).

Even if the deadline has passed, you should still file your state taxes as soon as possible to avoid penalties.
